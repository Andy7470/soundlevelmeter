Firstly, Explaning that this is a blog page, where i will to talk about the Sound level meter, also show the proces that i did to build this device from my experience, clarify this it is a project that i had been working before.

What is a sound level meter?

the sound level meter is a measuring instrument used to measure the sound pressure levels.

This mean that this will measure the levels of sound in a place, the unit to measure this is the decibels.

Image of a sound level meter.

Why use it?

This device is used in places where is important to keep a low levels of noise, like a Hospital, Libraries and Schools.
